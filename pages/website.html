UM DPII
From An Idea to Implementation

The design process started by asking a question: How can I improve a communication process?


When starting a new project I begin with a blank sheet, because I think in potentials rather than restrictions. With this particular project I was asked to design a website template for the department. I have done heavy research on other similar institutions to filter the important sections for the site. I strictly followed the color shcema requirements outlined in the brand guide of the university of Miami during the design process. I came up with a design that not only summarized the responsibilities of the department but also made it look modern and visually appealing.
