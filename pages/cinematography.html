Cinematography
Les Ondes
From An Idea to Implementation

The design process started by asking a question: How can I improve a communication process?


For this particular short film project I was asked to record a day of a person's life using a third-person narrative. The purpose of this project is to reveal emotion through planned cinematography. I have shot the actress in several location and used a wide range of cinematography techniques: wide shot, medium shot, close-up, extreme close-up and so on. I shot the film using Canon 5d mark three and edited, color corrected using Adobe Premiere Pro.
