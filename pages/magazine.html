Impact Magazine
Impactful Tech Trends
From An Idea to Implementation

The design process started by asking a question: How can I improve a communication process?


For this project I was asked to create a magazine template for a target audience at my choice. I am always enthusiastic about technology and media so I decided to go with a target audience that consists of young, tech-savvy, digital stylists. The entire magazine is broken down into the advertisement section, feature story section and latest tech news section. A wide range of colors were employed throughout the design of the magazine to add freshness and style to it.
