Informative Graphic Design
From An Idea to Implementation

The design process started by asking a question: How can I improve a communication process?


When starting a new project I begin with a blank sheet, because I think in potentials rather than restrictions. With this particular project I was asked to design a infographic summary of the new communication strategies for the department. I analyzed the important stakeholders and break them down into four categories:Surgeons, Patients, Coordinators and General Publics. For all stakeholders I had specific tactics to convey the information. I tried to get to the essence of the subject so we can develop strong concepts and intelligent solutions. We hit the books, do our research and collect relevant examples.
